Tutor FAQ

How do I schedule a lesson? ▼

Click "Book Trial Lesson" on any tutor's profile to view their real-time calendar availability. From there, you can select an open time slot that works best for your schedule and instantly confirm the booking.

Are the tutors background-checked? ▼

Yes, absolutely. The safety of our students is our top priority. All verified tutors undergo rigorous identity verification and comprehensive criminal background checks before they are allowed to list their services on our platform.

What happens if I'm not satisfied with my tutor? ▼

We offer a 100% Satisfaction Guarantee on your first lesson. If you don't feel it's a good fit, we'll gladly refund your session or credit your account so you can try another tutor completely risk-free.

What equipment do I need for online tutoring? ▼

You'll need a stable internet connection, a computer or tablet with a webcam, and a microphone. Our built-in digital classroom operates entirely in your web browser, so no special software downloads are required.
